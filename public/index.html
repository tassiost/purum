PROJECT_MANIFESTO //

Purum is a Bern-based DJ moving somewhere between groove-focused techno, house and chaotic late-night energy. His sets are built around tension, rhythm and unexpected turns — mixing hardgroove drums, old vocals, strange samples and hypnotic loops into something fast, raw and slightly surreal.

Influenced by underground clubs, vintage culture, internet nostalgia and futuristic visuals, the project goes beyond just DJing. Music, artwork, mixes and atmosphere all connect into the same world: dark, playful, cinematic and a bit unhinged. Less polished perfection, more feeling. Available for clubs, collectives, events and everything in between.
